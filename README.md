Doctor Strange — Mystic Arts Engine 🌀

A cinematic hand-tracking web experience inspired by the mystical magic effects of Doctor Strange.
This project uses real-time hand gesture recognition with MediaPipe Hands to generate magical visual effects such as portals, shields, smoke, sparks, and energy trails directly in the browser.

✨ Features
🖐 Real-Time Hand Tracking
Detects up to 2 hands simultaneously
Uses MediaPipe Hands
Smooth gesture tracking with high FPS rendering
Hand skeleton overlay visualization
☄ Mystic Energy Trail
Draw glowing energy trails using your index finger
Smooth Catmull-Rom spline interpolation
Multi-layer neon glow effect
🌫 Mystic Smoke System
Open palm gesture generates magical smoke particles
Dynamic particle movement with glowing cyan energy
Realistic floating animation
⚡ Spark Explosion Effect
Fist gesture creates explosive magical sparks
Particle physics with gravity simulation
Glowing ember trails
🌀 Sling Ring Portal
Bring two hands close together to open a portal
Animated rotating rings
Dynamic portal sparks and vortex effects
Cinematic energy glow
🛡 Mandala Shield
Open palm activates a rotating magical shield
Sacred geometry inspired design
Animated runes, concentric circles, rotating symbols
Pulsing mystical energy core
🌌 Cinematic Background
Infinite animated starfield
Space-like immersive atmosphere
Smooth parallax depth effect
📊 HUD System
Live effect indicators
Real-time FPS counter
Interactive status badges
